How to Install Packs

  1. Download your desired pack from the Downloads page.
  2. Open the .mcpack file directly with Minecraft Bedrock Edition.
  3. Go to Settings > Global Resources and activate the pack.
  4. Restart Minecraft to see the changes take effect.

Troubleshooting

Pack not working? Ensure you’re on the latest version of Minecraft Bedrock. Check for updates in the app store.

Download issues? Verify your internet connection or try re-downloading the file.
